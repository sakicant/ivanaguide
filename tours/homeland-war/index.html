Homeland War Experience

Recent history, told through the memories of someone who lived it.

Šibenik's recent history, told through the personal memories of a guide who experienced the Croatian Homeland War as a child.

This private walk tells the story of Šibenik during the Homeland War (1991–1995) - more than a history tour, it's a personal journey. As you walk the Old Town you'll learn how citizens defended their homes and how the city kept its identity, including the September 1991 Battle of Šibenik, alongside Ivana's own childhood memories of those years. She tells it with care, and with respect for what it means to the city.

Highlights

  • Old Town - how daily life continued during the war
  • Cathedral of St. James - a symbol of the city's resilience
  • Šibenik waterfront - the strategic importance of the coastline and harbour
  • The September 1991 Battle of Šibenik
  • The fortresses and their symbolic role in the city's defence
  • Life during the war - families, children, and community
