Old Town Private Walking Tour

Walk through Šibenik like a local.

Explore the historic heart of Šibenik beyond the main sights - the narrow stone streets, hidden corners, and atmospheric squares that reveal the city's true character.

Led by a licensed local guide, you'll trace centuries of history from medieval foundations to Venetian influences, hearing the stories behind landmarks like the Cathedral of St. James. Along the way Ivana shares personal insights, local legends, and glimpses of everyday life - a real connection to the city, not a memorised script.

Highlights

  • Cathedral of St. James - UNESCO Renaissance masterpiece (entrance ticket not included)
  • Šibenik waterfront - scenic seaside promenade
  • Old Town - a labyrinth of stone streets and squares
  • St. Barbara Church - home to the city's most unusual clock
  • City Hall - elegant Renaissance building on the main square
  • The hilltop fortresses, viewed from the Old Town with their history
