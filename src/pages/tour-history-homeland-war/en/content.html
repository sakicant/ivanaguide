History & Homeland War Private Tour

From medieval foundations to living memory - the full story of Šibenik.

The complete story of Šibenik - from medieval foundations to living memory - in one private walk.

This tour combines the highlights of the historic Old Town with a deeper look into the Croatian Homeland War, connecting centuries of history with the experiences of local people. Alongside the stories of kings, builders, and artists, Ivana shares her own childhood memories of growing up during the war - a personal connection to recent history you won't find in any guidebook.

Highlights

  • Cathedral of St. James - Croatia's Renaissance masterpiece
  • Old Town - centuries of history in stone streets and hidden corners
  • Šibenik waterfront - the sea, trade, and the city's strategic position
  • City Hall & St. Barbara Church with its unusual clock
  • The fortresses and their defensive role, including in the Homeland War
  • Šibenik during the Homeland War - through Ivana's own memories
