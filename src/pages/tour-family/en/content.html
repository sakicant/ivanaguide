Family Private Walking Tour

Discover Šibenik together, through a family-friendly local's eyes.

Turn your visit into a memorable family adventure on a private walking tour designed for parents and children.

Instead of dates and facts, this tour brings the city's history alive through engaging stories, fun discoveries, and interactive moments that keep younger visitors curious while adults enjoy the heritage. Because Ivana previously worked in a school, the storytelling adapts to your children's ages and interests - and the fully private format keeps the pace relaxed, with plenty of time for breaks, photos, and questions.

Highlights

  • Cathedral of St. James - famous UNESCO site, told through kid-friendly stories
  • Šibenik waterfront - local life and beautiful coastal views
  • Old Town - narrow streets, hidden passages, and picturesque squares
  • St. Barbara Church - the city's most unusual clock
  • City Hall - elegant Renaissance architecture
  • Stories of knights, battles, and the hilltop fortresses
