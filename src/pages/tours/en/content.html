Private walking tours of Šibenik

Every tour is fully private — only your group, led personally by Ivana, at your own pace. All prices are per group. All tours are wheelchair and stroller accessible and start at the same meeting point next to the INA gas station.

Old Town Private Walking Tour

Walk through Šibenik like a local.

Explore the historic heart of Šibenik beyond the main sights — the narrow stone streets, hidden corners, and atmospheric squares that reveal the city's true character. From medieval foundations to Venetian influences, you'll hear the stories behind the landmarks and everyday life in the city.

Highlights

  • Cathedral of St. James — UNESCO Renaissance masterpiece (entrance ticket not included)
  • Šibenik waterfront — scenic seaside promenade
  • Old Town — a labyrinth of stone streets and squares
  • St. Barbara Church — home to the city's most unusual clock
  • City Hall — elegant Renaissance building on the main square
  • The hilltop fortresses, viewed from the Old Town with their history

Homeland War Experience

Recent history, told through the memories of someone who lived it.

A private walking tour about Šibenik during the Croatian Homeland War (1991–1995) — more than a history tour, it's a personal journey through the childhood memories of your guide, who experienced the war as a child. Alongside key events like the September Battle of 1991, you'll learn how citizens defended their homes and how the city preserved its strength and identity.

2–2.5 hours
Price

Highlights

  • Life during the war — families, children, and community
