History & Coffee with a Local Guide

A walk through centuries, then a conversation over coffee.

A private walk through centuries of history, followed by a relaxed coffee and conversation with your guide.

Explore the historic heart of Šibenik - medieval streets, Renaissance squares, and the UNESCO Cathedral of St. James - then take a break at a local café. Over coffee the conversation continues: ask questions, hear personal stories, and get authentic recommendations on food, beaches, and hidden places that only a local can share.

Highlights

  • Cathedral of St. James - UNESCO Renaissance masterpiece
  • Old Town - stone streets, historic squares, Dalmatian atmosphere
  • City Hall & St. Barbara Church with its hard-to-read clock
  • The fortresses and their role, including during the Homeland War
  • Venetian heritage & medieval history
  • A relaxed coffee break with local recommendations
