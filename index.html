Welcome

A city best seen through local eyes

Šibenik isn't a museum you pass through - it's a living Dalmatian city with a thousand years of stories folded into its stone streets. On a private walk with Ivana you'll meet the Šibenik behind the postcards: the legends, the small details, and the everyday life that no group tour ever slows down for.

Your guide

Meet Ivana

Ivana is a licensed local guide, officially certified by the Croatian Ministry of Tourism, born and raised in Šibenik. Before guiding full time she worked in a school - which is exactly why her tours land just as well with curious children as with history-loving adults.

She shares the city not only as a destination, but as the place she calls home: the legends, the quiet corners, and, on the Homeland War tours, her own childhood memories of the early 1990s.
